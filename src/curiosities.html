CURIOSITIES

  • The show was created, sold, and written in just one day;
  • Originally, Rick and Morty were parodies of Doc Brown and Marty McFly from Back to the Future;
  • The episode "Look Who's Purging Now", inspired by The Purge, was made improvisationally and no one had seen the movie;
  • The creators were inspired by Ren & Stimpy, Beavis and Butt-Head, South Park, and others;
  • Adult Swim gives the creators total freedom;
  • The inspiration for Mr. Meeseeks came from a cat;
  • The mind parasites were inspired by Buffy the Vampire Slayer;
  • All of Rick's burps are real and were created accidentally;
  • Pickle Rick is a tribute to the "4 Days Out" episode of Breaking Bad;
  • Gearhead is inspired by Roboto from He-Man and the flying saucer is inspired by Calvin and Hobbes;
  • The writers do not research the science part of the series;
  • "Wubba lubba dub dub" was a failed attempt to imitate The Three Stooges;
  • Justin Roiland gets drunk to voice Rick.
